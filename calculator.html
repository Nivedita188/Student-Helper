Letter grade and the numerical equivalents used for this calculator

Grade point average (GPA) is a commonly used indicator of an individual's academic achievement in college. It is the average of the grades attained in each course, taking course credit into consideration. This calculator accepts letter grades and credit of the particular subject. These letter grades are translated into numerical values as shown below.

  • A+ = 10 grade points
  • A = 9 grade points
  • B+ = 8 grade points
  • B = 7 grade points
  • C+ = 6 grade points
  • C = 5 points
  • D+ = 4 grade points
  • D = 3 grade point
  • E+ = 2 grade point

GPA calculation formula

GPA = Σ (grade value x unit credit points) ÷ Σ unit credit points
Calculation steps

  • Multiply each grade value by the unit credit points.
  • Sum the resulting values (weighted GPA unit score).
  • Sum the unit credit points
  • Divide the sum of the weighted GPA unit score by the sum of the unit credit points
  • Calculate to three decimal places.
